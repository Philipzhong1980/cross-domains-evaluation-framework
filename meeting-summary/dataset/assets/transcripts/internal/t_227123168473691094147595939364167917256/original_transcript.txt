Harsha Ramchandani: In recent years, the City has received approximately 620,000 in CDBG funds and approximately 295,000 in home funds annually.

Harsha Ramchandani: For capital projects, which typically have much greater fundings, it is critical that the City uses federal funds thoughtfully and administers the funds effectively and efficiently.
