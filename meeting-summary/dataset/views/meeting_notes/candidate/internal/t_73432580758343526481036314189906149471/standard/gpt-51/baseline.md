### Overview
The Mountain View City Council convenes a virtual meeting with Mayor Lucas Ramirez presiding and City Attorney Danica Lowe outlining the scope of a scheduled closed session. Lowe explains that the Council will conduct public employee performance evaluations for the City Attorney, City Clerk, and City Manager under California Government Code Section 54957 and indicates that no reportable action will result from the session. Ramirez then opens the floor for public comment on the closed session item, explains how to request to speak, and, after confirming that no members of the public seek to comment, formally recesses the Council into closed session.
