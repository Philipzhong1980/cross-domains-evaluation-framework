### Overview
The Joint Administrative, Zoning and Subdivision Committee Public Hearing on November 23, 2022, led by Diana Pancholi, focused on a development review permit for a new office building at 1155 and 1185 Terabella Ave. Brittany Whitehill presented the project, which includes a lot line adjustment and the removal of three heritage trees, confirming its compliance with zoning regulations. Public comments were solicited, with Weng Tam advocating for local hiring standards. The committee deliberated and unanimously approved both the lot line adjustment and the development review permit, with decisions being appealable to the City Council.
