#### Annual Sewer Main Replacement Project
Tony Eagles from Public Works presented on the upcoming sewer main replacement on Castro Street and Wild Cherry Lane, detailing construction methods including open trench, spot repairs, and pipe bursting. Nighttime work is planned to minimize business impact, with some daytime work possible. Noise, traffic detours, and communication with affected businesses and residents were discussed. The project is expected to start in summer with duration dependent on contractor scheduling and coordination with other projects.
- Tony Eagles explains pipe bursting as a method to replace pipes by breaking old pipe while pulling new pipe through.
- Nighttime construction from 11 PM to 6 AM is planned to reduce business disruption.
- Open trench work will occur at the El Camino Real intersection during the day under Caltrans permit.
- Communication plans include notifying businesses and residents about schedules and impacts.
- Noise concerns, especially near residences and nightclubs, are acknowledged with plans for outreach.
#### ARPA Funds for Small Business Economic Vitality
John presented proposed programs for allocating $500,000 in ARPA funds to support small businesses, including facade matching grants, outdoor furnishings grants, a contract amendment for the Rethink Disposable program, and a social media partnership with the Chamber of Commerce. The programs aim to improve business aesthetics, sustainability, and marketing capabilities. Committee members discussed social media challenges and training needs for businesses.
- Facade matching grants offer one-to-one matching funds for property improvements with three tiers of funding.
- Outdoor furnishings grants provide $5,000 for replacing outdoor fixtures citywide.
- Rethink Disposable program promotes reusable food ware to reduce waste in food service businesses.
- A $25,000 partnership with the Chamber will develop social media toolkits and training for small businesses.
- Mary Yen and Kira Pasca emphasize the importance of social media training and peer learning for business owners.
