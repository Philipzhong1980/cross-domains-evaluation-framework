### Overview
The Senior Advisory Committee meeting held on January 18, 2023, focused on several key topics including the approval of previous minutes, the election of Deanne Hui as Chair and Elna Tymes as Vice Chair for the year, and updates from various subcommittees. Ish Sinha proposed a friendly visitors program to address senior loneliness, while Vicki Gilfix and Elna Tymes provided updates on the Senior Center and survey initiatives, respectively. Ashley Bittle reported on upcoming Senior Center activities, including workshops and a pancake breakfast, and Jim Nichols raised concerns about transportation access for seniors. The meeting concluded with announcements of a benefit concert and a reminder for the next in-person meeting.

### Detailed Summary
