### Overview
The City Council held a special meeting to interview applicants for the Environmental Planning Commission, utilizing a structured format to ensure fairness. After a public comment period with no engagement, the council proceeded with interviews of seven candidates, each sharing their backgrounds and perspectives on community issues and city planning. Key discussions included the need for revisions to the city's General Plan and balancing private property rights with community benefits. Following deliberation, the council recommended Bill Cranston and Jose Gutierrez for reappointment, recognizing their contributions and commitment to the community.
