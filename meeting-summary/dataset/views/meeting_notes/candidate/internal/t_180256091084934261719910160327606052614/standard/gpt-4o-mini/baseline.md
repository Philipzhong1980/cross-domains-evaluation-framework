### Overview
The Development Review Committee meeting on November 2nd focused on the 500 Ellis St. project, which proposes a hotel and office building in the East Wisman neighborhood. Tom Jess from Eris Studio Architects presented the project, highlighting its design features, including a mechanical parking system and tree preservation efforts. The committee engaged in discussions about the project's pedestrian orientation, architectural aesthetics, and public feedback. Key recommendations included enhancing the building's facade, improving bike parking accessibility, and collaborating with staff on design revisions. The committee expressed appreciation for the design team's responsiveness and encouraged further refinements ahead of the upcoming public hearings.
