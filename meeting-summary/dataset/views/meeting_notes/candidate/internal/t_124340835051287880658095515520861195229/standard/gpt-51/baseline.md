### Overview
The January 9, 2023 Mountain View Rental Housing Committee meeting, chaired by Haines-Livesay with members Rosas, Almond, and Vice Chair Ramos present and members Grunwald and Pardo Dazella absent, focuses on routine governance and upcoming leadership and scheduling changes. The committee first approves the consent calendar, with Almond moving and Ramos seconding a resolution to continue holding virtual meetings under the Brown Act as amended by AB 361, which passes unanimously without public comment. During oral communications, Chair Haines-Livesay invites public comment on non-agenda items, but no speakers come forward. She then announces that the end-of-January meeting will be her last due to no longer meeting the residency requirement, prompting appreciative remarks from Rosas, Almond, Ramos, and staff member Anky van Deursen recognizing her service as chair and former vice chair. Finally, in response to Almond’s question about the next meeting, Anky van Deursen explains that a new City Council meeting on January 30 creates a conflict, so staff will poll committee members about rescheduling, and the meeting adjourns with the January session tentatively set for January 30 at 7:00 PM, subject to a possible date change to be announced.
