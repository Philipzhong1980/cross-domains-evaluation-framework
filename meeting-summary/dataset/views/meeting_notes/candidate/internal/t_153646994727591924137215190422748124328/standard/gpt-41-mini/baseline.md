### Overview
The Joint Administrative Zoning and Subdivision Committee convened to review three key land use items. At 339 Franklin Street, the committee approved a planned community permit, development review permit, heritage tree removal, and a lot line adjustment to facilitate construction of a new single-family home, with applicant Jenny Gong emphasizing neighborhood improvement. The committee then unanimously approved a one-year extension for a vesting tentative map at 1411 to 1495 West El Camino Real, allowing the developer additional time to prepare construction documents. Finally, a lot line adjustment at 890 Central Avenue was approved, ensuring compliance with zoning and land use standards. Throughout, staff presentations by Ella Carraccian and Brittany Whitehill provided detailed project analyses, and committee members Ed Arango and Diana Fizely actively participated in motions and approvals. All items are subject to appeal to the City Council within 10 calendar days.
  
### Detailed Summary
#### 339 Franklin St Project Review
The committee reviewed a project at 339 Franklin Street involving the construction of a 500 square foot single-family home, a heritage tree removal, and a lot line adjustment to combine two lots. The project aligns with the Downtown Precise Plan and General Plan, supporting neighborhood transition and density standards. The heritage tree removal was justified due to the tree's poor health and risk to adjacent homes, and the lot line adjustment facilitates the development.
- Ella Carraccian explained the project includes a planned community permit, development review permit, heritage tree removal, and lot line adjustment.
- Ella Carraccian noted the heritage tree removal is necessary due to the tree's structural risks and root loss from construction.
- Stephanie Williams approved the planned community permit, development review permit, and heritage tree removal based on staff recommendations.
- Ed Arango motioned to approve the lot line adjustment, seconded by Diana Fizely, with unanimous committee approval.
- Jenny Gong, the applicant, stated the new home will improve the neighborhood by replacing an uninhabitable duplex.
#### 1411-1495 W El Camino Real Extension
The committee considered a one-year extension request for a vesting tentative map to create 53 condominium lots and one common lot on a 1.25-acre site. The project complies with the El Camino Real Precise Plan and is categorically exempt from SEQUA review. The extension allows the applicant additional time to prepare construction documents and permits.
- Brittany Whitehill presented the extension request and confirmed consistency with planning guidelines.
- Steve Saray from Lux Larva Development expressed readiness to answer questions and explained the need for the extension to finalize permits.
- Diana Fizely moved to approve the one-year extension, seconded by Ed Arango, with unanimous committee approval.
#### 890 Central Ave Lot Line Adjustment
The committee reviewed a lot line adjustment proposal at 890 Central Avenue to relocate a boundary between two existing lots on a 0.44-acre site. The adjustment complies with R1 zoning standards and the low-density residential land use designation. The project is categorically exempt from SEQUA review and recommended for conditional approval.
- Brittany Whitehill presented the lot line adjustment and confirmed compliance with zoning and land use plans.
- No public comments were received on this item.
- Ed Arango moved to approve the lot line adjustment subject to conditions, seconded by Diana Fizely, with unanimous committee approval.
