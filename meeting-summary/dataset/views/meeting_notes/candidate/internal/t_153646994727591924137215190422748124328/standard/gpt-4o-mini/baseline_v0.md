### Overview
The Joint Administrative Zoning and Subdivision Committee convened on October 12, 2022, led by Zoning Administrator Stephanie Williams, with key participants including Ed Arango and Diana Fizely. The meeting addressed public communications, approved a consent calendar, and reviewed three main agenda items: a project at 339 Franklin St involving a new single-family home and heritage tree removal, a one-year extension for a condominium project on W El Camino Real, and a lot line adjustment at 890 Central Ave. All items received unanimous approvals, with the committee confirming compliance with local regulations and noting appeal options to the City Council.

### Detailed Summary
#### Public Communications
The meeting begins with an opportunity for public comments, allowing individuals to address the zoning administrator on any non-agenda items. No public speakers are present, leading to the next agenda item.
- Stephanie Williams opens the floor for public comments, stating that speakers are limited to three minutes.
- No members of the public choose to speak during this time.

#### Consent Calendar Approval
The consent calendar, which includes an item at 2134 Old Middlefield Way, is presented for approval. With no public comments, the calendar is approved unanimously, and the item is noted as appealable to the City Council within ten days.
- Stephanie Williams states that the consent calendar will be approved in one motion unless pulled for discussion.
- The consent calendar is approved without any public comments.

#### 339 Franklin St Project Review
The committee reviews a project at 339 Franklin St, which involves a planned community permit, development review permit, and a heritage tree removal permit. The project aims to construct a new single-family home and remove a heritage tree due to health concerns, with the project deemed consistent with local plans.
- Ella Carraccian presents the project details, highlighting the need for a heritage tree removal due to its unstable condition.
- Jenny Gong, the applicant, supports the project by emphasizing the current structure's poor condition.

#### Approval of 339 Franklin St Project
After public comments, the committee deliberates on the project, approving the planned community permit and the lot line adjustment. The decision is based on staff recommendations and compliance with local regulations.
- Stephanie Williams confirms the approval of the planned community permit and heritage tree removal.
- Ed Arango motions to approve the lot line adjustment, which is seconded by Diana Fizely, leading to unanimous approval.

#### 1411 to 1495 W El Camino Real Extension
The committee discusses a request for a one-year extension of a previously approved vesting tentative map for a condominium project on W El Camino Real. Staff finds the extension consistent with local plans and recommends approval.
- Brittany Whitehill presents the request for a one-year extension, stating it is consistent with the El Camino Real Precise Plan.
- Diana Fizely motions to approve the extension, which is seconded and unanimously approved.

#### 890 Central Ave Lot Line Adjustment
The final item involves a lot line adjustment at 890 Central Ave, which is found to be consistent with zoning standards. The committee reviews the proposal and recommends conditional approval.
- Brittany Whitehill outlines the details of the lot line adjustment, confirming its compliance with zoning regulations.
- Ed Arango motions to approve the lot line adjustment, which is seconded and unanimously approved.
