### Overview
The December 14, 2022 administrative zoning public hearing, presided over by Zoning Administrator Lindsay Hagan, addressed two main agenda items. The consent calendar included a continued development agreement request for 1040 Terabella Ave, which was deferred without action, and a permit request for 2500 Grant Rd involving removal of ten trees, including heritage trees, which was approved with conditions after staff and the administrator reviewed and agreed on the tree removal and replacement plans. The public hearing focused on a provisional use permit for a new medical service use (Carbon Health) at 2580 W El Camino Real, with staff recommending approval based on consistency with the San Antonio Precise Plan and General Plan. No public comments were received on any items. The zoning administrator approved the medical service use with conditions and reminded attendees of the appeal process. The meeting concluded promptly with all actions documented and no outstanding concerns raised.
  
### Detailed Summary
#### Consent Calendar Items
The meeting reviewed two consent calendar items: a development agreement request for 1040 Terabella Ave, which was continued to a future date, and a permit request for 2500 Grant Rd involving tree removals and landscape modifications. The 1040 Terabella Ave item was deferred with no action taken, while the 2500 Grant Rd item was approved with conditions after staff and the zoning administrator concurred on the tree removal and replacement plans.
- Zoning Administrator Lindsay Hagan stated that item 3.1 for 1040 Terabella Ave is continued to a future date with no action taken.
- Lindsay Hagan approved item 3.2 for 2500 Grant Rd, agreeing with the tree removal and replacement plan as presented in the staff materials.
- No public comments were received on either consent calendar item.
#### Public Hearing for 2580 W El Camino Real
A public hearing was held for a provisional use permit to allow a new medical service use (Carbon Health) in an existing commercial building at 2580 W El Camino Real, along with permits to replace awnings. Staff recommended conditional approval, finding the project consistent with the San Antonio Precise Plan and General Plan. No public comments were received, and the zoning administrator approved the project with conditions.
- Project planner Phillip Brennan presented the proposal and recommended conditional approval based on consistency with planning guidelines.
- Applicant representatives were present but did not provide additional comments.
- Zoning Administrator Lindsay Hagan concurred with staff's recommendation and approved the project with posted conditions.
- No public comments were made during the hearing on this item.
