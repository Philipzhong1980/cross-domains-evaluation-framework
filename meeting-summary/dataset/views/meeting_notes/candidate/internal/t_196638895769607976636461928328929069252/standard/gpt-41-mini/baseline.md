### Overview
The Performing Arts Committee meeting, chaired by Vice Chair Sukanya Kumar with key contributions from staff members Noelle Magner and Theresa Yvonne, focused on several substantive agenda items. The committee approved corrected minutes from the previous meeting, ensuring accurate titles were used. An update on the Mountain View Film Festival subcommittee highlighted ongoing planning efforts and budget preparations for a potential 2023 festival. The annual review of Peninsula Youth Theatre (PYT) was a major focus, with staff clarifying financial details and PYT's Director of Business Operations, Connor Day, presenting a comprehensive overview of the past season's programming, outreach, and challenges, including COVID-19 impacts and audience rebuilding. Discussions included financial reporting improvements and operational policies such as masking and vaccination. Staff announcements included the upcoming National Arts and Humanities Proclamation presentation and plans to explore performing arts conferences to inform future programming decisions. The meeting concluded with acknowledgments of recent city volunteer events and scheduling of the next meeting featuring additional home company presentations.

#### Peninsula Youth Theatre Annual Review
The committee reviewed the Peninsula Youth Theatre's (PYT) annual performance, financials, and outreach activities. Staff presented a summary of ticket sales, facility charges, and expenses, clarifying terminology and financial details. PYT's Director of Business Operations, Connor Day, provided an overview of the past season's programming, outreach efforts, and challenges, including COVID-19 impacts and audience rebuilding. The committee discussed financial reporting improvements and the need for broader budget context including overhead costs. PYT highlighted successful school outreach, sold-out performances, summer camps, and funding sources. Masking and vaccination policies were also discussed, along with challenges in staffing and audience development.
- Noelle Magner clarified financial spreadsheet abbreviations and charges related to PYT's use of the facility.
- Theresa Yvonne explained the net revenue and facility fees paid by PYT, excluding overhead costs.
- John McAlister requested more comprehensive financial reports including overhead and budget context, which staff committed to providing in future meetings.
- Connor Day detailed PYT's outreach programs, including School Play in a Box and Stories on Stage, noting COVID-19 impacts and recent successes with sold-out school performances.
- Connor Day described mainstage musicals, summer camps, and donor support including new state grants.
- Masking and vaccination policies were discussed, with PYT maintaining some requirements and receiving mostly supportive feedback from parents.
- Challenges include rebuilding audiences, technical staff shortages, and balancing volunteer labor with paid staff.
- Committee members were invited to attend upcoming PYT performances and encouraged to support and refer technical staff.
