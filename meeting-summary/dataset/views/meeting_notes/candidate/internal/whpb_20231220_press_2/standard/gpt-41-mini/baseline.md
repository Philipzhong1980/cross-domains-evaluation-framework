### Overview
The meeting featured key updates and discussions led by MS. JEAN-PIERRE and MR. KIRBY, covering a broad range of topics including President Biden's upcoming visit to Milwaukee to promote Black-owned small businesses and infrastructure investments under the Biden administration. Humanitarian aid efforts in Gaza were detailed, highlighting significant truck deliveries and the opening of new aid routes. The administration addressed ongoing hostage negotiations and emphasized the priority of securing the safe return of detained Americans. U.S. positions on the Gaza conflict, including civilian casualty concerns and U.N. Security Council resolutions, were outlined.
