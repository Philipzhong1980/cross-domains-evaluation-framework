### Overview
The Visual Arts Committee meeting on November 9, 2022, was led by Toni Hsu and included key participants such as John Lang and Jesse Cupp. The meeting covered several important topics, including the approval of previous meeting minutes, public communications from local artists, and updates on Capital Improvement Program (CIP) public art projects. The committee discussed upcoming agenda items, including a comprehensive approach to public improvement projects and the public art strategy. John Lang provided updates on the status of various CIP projects, emphasizing the need for better integration of public art with park elements. The meeting concluded with an update on the Call for Artists for the Center for the Performing Arts, with 29 submissions received and a call for committee members to review them before the next meeting.
